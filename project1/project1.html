About Shanghai

The Largest City in China

Shanghai is seen as China's largest city and has the 3rd largest city population in the world. As of 2025, Shanghai has a population of 30.4 million with a blend of eastern and westerns culture and influence. Shanghai is also seen as the richest city in China with the tallest building in Shanghai known as the Shanghai Tower being the 3rd tallest building overall in the world. Shanghai also has many nicknames like: "New York of China", "Pearl of the Orient", "Magical City", and "Pearis of the East".
