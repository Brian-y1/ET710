City in china: Shanghai

Shagnhai is china's largest city with a population of over 24.9 Million. It is a city that looks beautiful at night with all the colors from each building. Shanghai has many nicknames like "The magic city", "The new york of China", and "The Pearl of the Orident". A city known for its Cultural mix, architecture, and food. It also hold it self as a major global financial center. It is also known for places like: Oriental Pearl Tower, Jade Buddha Temple, longhua temple and the city god temple in shanghai.

More about Shanghai

Early History

Shanghia during its early year began as a small fishing village during the Qin and Han dynasties.During the Jin dynasty it started to develope into a port.

World War 1

Shanghai experienced a increase in foreign trade. China in 1917 declared neutrality during the world war and sent people over to Britain, France, and Russia. Shagnhai benefited as a major port during the war and became a place for goods and supplies

World War 2

During the 2nd world war. Shanghai became a battlefeild between China and Japan. It is considered the First major battle during the second sino-japanese war and was also refered to by some historians as the first battle during world war 2.

Great Depression

Shanghai during the great depression suffered heavily with Shanghai's Production decreasing and it's ecnonmy becomeing destabilized. During this time Shanghai also suffered from floods from the Yangtze river

Opium War

Shanghai had major transformations during the opium war. Before the start of the opium war, Shanhai was a small agricultural village. By the time the First opium war ended with the treaty of Nanjing, china opened five treaty ports with shanghai being one of the 5 ports open.
