City in china: Shanghai

Shagnhai is china's largest city with a population of over 24.9 Million. It is a city that looks beautiful at night with all the colors from each building. Shanghai has many nicknames like "The magic city", "The new york of China", and "The Pearl of the Orient". A city known for its Cultural mix, architecture, and food. It also hold it self as a major global financial center. It is also known for places like: Oriental Pearl Tower, Jade Buddha Temple, longhua temple and the city god temple in shanghai.
