History of shanghai

When Shanghai was first founded, it was a small fishing village and market town located near Yangzi river. It started to turn into a county capital during the times of the yuan dynasty. It turned into a trading port after the events of the opium war. By the time world war 2 arrived, it was under the control of japan. This event brough about a time of conflict and hardships. By the time we reached modern years, Shanghai became a cosmopolitan city and a imporatnt major city in china.

Events that changed Shanghai

Early History

Shanghia during its early year began as a small fishing village during the Qin and Han dynasties.During the Jin dynasty it started to develope into a port.

World War 1

Shanghai experienced a increase in foreign trade when China in 1917 declared neutrality during the world war. Shagnhai benefited as a major port during the war and became a place for goods and supplies

World War 2

During the 2nd world war. Shanghai became a battlefeild between China and Japan. It is considered the First major battle during the second Sino-japanese war and argued as the first battle of World War 2.

Great Depression

Shanghai during the great depression suffered heavily with Shanghai's Production decreasing and it's ecnonmy became destabilized, They also suffered from floods.

Opium War

Shanghai had major transformations during the opium war. By the time the First opium war ended with the treaty of Nanjing, Shanghai became one of the 5 ports that was opened.
