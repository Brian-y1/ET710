Learn and Visit

You've probably heard atleast one of these historical monuments before throughout your time in school. From the Statue of Liberty to monuments dedicated to presidents like George Washington, Abraham Lincoln, Tomas Jefferson, and Theodore Roosevelt. Come have a chance at seeing these amazing historical monuments close up and learn about the importance behind these monuments and the dedication, love, and care placed into preserving these monuments all the way up to modern times.

About the National Park Service

The National Park Service is part of the U.S. Department of the Interior and is tasked with preserving and protecting Historcial Monuments through the help of over 20,000 workers.They pride themselves with managing and preserving national parks and helping American Communities.
