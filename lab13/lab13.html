Rubiks Cube

Form of Entertainment

A Rubiks Cube is a cube with each side having one specific color. The goal of the Rubik's cube is to mix the cube and try to reoriant the cube back to it's original form.

More about Rubik's Cube

The standard and traditional Rubik's cube is held in a the form of a 3 by 3. Although from its looks it looks simple, its acually of the the most challenging puzzles to solve. What makes it so challening is the the fact the rubiks cube is only solvable using whats is refered to as algorithms. These are sets of ordered moves that are used to assist in solving the rubiks cube.
