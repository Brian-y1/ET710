A traditional Rubik's cube is a 3x3.It was first made to help students understand 3d shapes. However a someone came and say that it had potential in the toy market. It was first called the magic cube however when it was being marketed as a toy it was renamed the Rubik's cube coming from the creators name Erno Rubik. It acually took him one whole month for him to solve it the fist time when he created it. There are competitive sports surrounding Rubik's cube called speed cubbing, it is a term where people attempt to solve a rubik's cube as fast as porssible given set amount of time to inspect the cube and a random scramble not by the person. Before times like 1 minute was acually considered fast, However overtime as people amount more efficient ways to solve it and betters cubes came out the times to solve has changed a lot with present days solve being around 10 seconds or even less. People also expanded on the design of rubiks, now there are different variations of a rubik's cube, some are the triangle cube, 5 by 5 cube, 20 by 20 cube, and even 100 by 100 cubes.
